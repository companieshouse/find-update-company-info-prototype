You need to add an account owner

Authorised agents must have at least one account owner.

You need to add an account owner to MORRIS ACCOUNTING LTD before you remove yourself from the account.

Why authorised agents must have an account owner

The authorised agent is legally required tell us if there is a change to any of the details we hold about the business within 14 days.

If there are changes, an account owner must update the details in the authorised agent account.

Account owners are also the only users who can:

  • close the authorised agent account, if the business is no longer an authorised agent
  • add and remove other account owners
