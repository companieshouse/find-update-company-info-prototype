Before you add a user, check that they have a Companies House account. If they do not, they will need to create one first.

How to check if someone has a Companies House account

Ask them if they already have sign in details for the Companies House register. This is sometimes known as 'Find and update company information', or the 'Companies House service.

It would be the same details they use for some of our online services, such as 'Register your company' or 'Upload a document to Companies House'.

A Companies House account is different to WebFiling, which is the service to submit certain filings digitally, such as dormant accounts.
