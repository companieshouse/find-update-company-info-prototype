Before you add a user

To add someone as a user, they must already have a Companies House account. This is the account they would use to sign in to:

  • the Companies House register - sometimes known as 'Find and update company information', or the 'Companies House service
  • some of our online services, such as 'Register your company' or 'Upload a document to Companies House'
A Companies House account is different to WebFiling, which is the service to submit certain filings digitally, such as dormant accounts.
If they do not have a Companies House account

Ask them to create sign in details for Companies House (opens in a new tab).

They will need to do this before you add them.
