What is a Companies House account?

You use your Companies House account to sign in to the Companies House register. This is sometimes known as 'Find and update company information', or the 'Companies House service.

You can also use it for some of our online services, such as 'Register your company' or 'Upload a document to Companies House'.

A Companies House account is different to WebFiling, which is the service to submit certain filings digitally, such as dormant accounts.
