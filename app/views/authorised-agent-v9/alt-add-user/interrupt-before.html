Before you add a user

To add someone as a user, they must already have a Companies House account. This is different to a WebFiling account.

A Companies House account is used to sign in to:

  • the Companies House register - sometimes known as 'Find and update company information', or the 'Companies House service'
  • some of our online services, such as 'Upload a document to Companies House'
If the user does not have a Companies House account

Ask them to create sign in details for Companies House (opens in a new tab).

The user will need to do this before you add them.
