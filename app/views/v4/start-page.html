We have changed the way you sign in to Companies House

We’ll ask you to create a GOV.UK One Login, which is the new way to sign in to government services. In the future, it will allow you to access any government service using the same email address and password.

If you already have a GOV.UK One Login, you’ll be able to sign in.

You can save your existing information

If you have an existing WebFiling or Companies House account, you may have information stored, such as:

  • email reminders
  • details of companies you file for
  • your filing history
  • companies you follow

If you want to save this information, we'll ask you to enter the email address and password you use to sign in to these accounts.

What is WebFiling?

This is an online service you can use to submit certain filings digitally, such as dormant accounts.

In WebFiling, you can also:

  • sign up for email reminders
  • request or change your authentication code
What is a Companies House account?

This is the account you can use to sign in to:

  • the Companies House register - sometimes known as 'Companies House service' or 'Find and update company information'
  • some of our online services - for example, 'Register your company' or 'Upload a document to Companies House'
