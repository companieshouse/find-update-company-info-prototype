We have changed the way you sign in

We’ll ask you to create a GOV.UK One Login, which is the new way to sign in to many government services. In the future, it will allow you to access any government service using the same email address and password.

You can save your existing information

If you have an existing Companies House account, you may have information stored, such as:

  • your filing history
  • companies you follow

To save your information, enter the email address you use to sign in to this account when you create your GOV.UK One Login.

If you already have a GOV.UK One Login

You’ll be able to sign in. If you created your GOV.UK One Login using your Companies House account email address, you'll be able to access your existing information when you sign in.

If you’ve used a different email address, you can create a new GOV.UK One Login using your Companies House account email address to access your existing information.

What is a Companies House account?

This is the account you can use to sign in to:

  • the Companies House register - sometimes known as 'Companies House service' or 'Find and update company information'
  • some of our online services - for example, 'Register your company' or 'Upload a document to Companies House'
