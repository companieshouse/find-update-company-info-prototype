You are no longer digitally authorised to file for companies that were saved to your account

You may have saved companies to your account so you no longer need to enter the authenication code when filing for a company. However, Companies House requires users to re-enter the authentication code to file for a company, or be digitally authorised by another person, before they file for the company.

! Warning You will need to enter the authenication code or become digitally authorised in order to file for a company.

Enter the authentication code when you file

You can save a company to your account when you next file for a company.

Review the list of Your companies in your account

If you file for multiple companies, you may want to review the list of companies within your account. You can:

  • your filing history
  • companies you follow
What is a Companies House account?

This is the account you can use to sign in to:

  • the Companies House register - sometimes known as 'Companies House service' or 'Find and update company information'
  • some of our online services - for example, 'Register your company' or 'Upload a document to Companies House'

A Companies House account is not the same as WebFiling.
