What to do if you already have a GOV.UK One Login that uses a different email address

To connect your account with GOV.UK One Login, you must use the same email address for both.

The GOV.UK One Login email222@example.com, does not match your Companies House account email address, which is email111@example.com.

If you want to connect this Companies House account to a GOV.UK One Login that uses a different email address, you have 2 options:

Option 1

Change your Companies House account email address to match your GOV.UK One Login email address. Then go back to the ‘Manage account’ section and select continue.

Option 2

Sign in to GOV.UK One Login and change the email address to email111@example.com (opens in new tab), which is your Companies House account email address. Then go back to the ‘Manage account’ section and select continue.
