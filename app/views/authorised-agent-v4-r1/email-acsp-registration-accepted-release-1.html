Register as an authorised agent: application approved

We have approved your application and registered MORRIS ACCOUNTING LTD (02050399) as a Companies House authorised agent. This is also known as an Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP).

How to use the authorised agent account

You can access the authorised agent account for MORRIS ACCOUNTING LTD when you sign in to your Companies House account.

1. Add users to the account

If other people who work for the business need to file with Companies House, you should add them to the account so they can access services for authorised agents. They do not need to verify their identity.

Sign in to your Companies House account to add users to MORRIS ACCOUNTING LTD:

https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk

2. Comply with your legal responsibilities

You must tell us within 14 days if there is a change to the Anti-Money Laundering (AML):

  • registration status
  • supervisory body
  • membership number or ID

You can do this in the 'Update details' section of the agent account.

Read the full list of legal responsibilities for authorised agents: htts://gov.uk/guidance-apply-to-register-as-an-authorised-agent#legal-responsibilities

3. Use services for authorised agents

In the future, all users who have been added to the agent account will be able to:

  • file on behalf of clients
  • verify people's identity for Companies House
