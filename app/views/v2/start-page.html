We have changed the way you sign in to Companies House

We’ll ask you to create a GOV.UK One Login, which is the new way to sign in to government services. In the future, it will allow you to access any government service using the same email address and password.

If you already have a GOV.UK One Login, you’ll be able to sign in.

What will happen to my existing information?

If you have a Companies House account, you may have information stored, such as:

  • a record of your filing history
  • companies you follow

We'll ask you if you want us to save this information in your GOV.UK One Login account.
