Email addresses must match

To connect your Companies House account to your GOV.UK One Login, you must use the same email address for both. You have 2 options.

Option 1

Change your Companies House account email address

  1. Go to ‘Change email address’ (opens in a new tab).
  2. Change your Companies House account email address to {{data['one-login-email']}}.
  3. Select ‘Manage account’.
  4. Select continue to connect your account to your GOV.UK One Login.

Option 2

Change your GOV.UK One Login email address

  1. Sign in to your GOV.UK One Login (opens in a new tab).
  2. Select ‘Security’.
  3. Change your GOV.UK One Login email address to {{ data['chs-email'] }}.
  4. Go back to your Companies House account and select ‘Manage account’.
  5. Select continue to connect your account to your GOV.UK One Login.
