Sign in to Companies House

From now on, everyone must sign in to Companies House using GOV.UK One Login. You'll be able to create a GOV.UK One Login if you do not already have one. Read more about the changes to signing in to Companies House.

You can save your existing information

If you use the same email address for your GOV.UK One Login as your existing Companies House account, we'll save any information stored, such as:

  • your filing history
  • companies you follow
