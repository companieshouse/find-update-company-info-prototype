How you sign in to Companies House has changed

Read more about the changes to signing in to Companies House.

Sign in to Companies House

From now on, you'll sign in to your Companies House account using GOV.UK One Login. You'll be able to create a GOV.UK One Login if you do not already have one.

You can save your existing information

If you use the same email address for your GOV.UK One Login as your existing Companies House account, we'll save any information stored, such as:

  • your filing history
  • companies you follow
