Sign in with GOV.UK One Login

This is the new way to sign in to Companies House. You'll be able to create a GOV.UK One Login if you do not already have one.

{{ govukButton({ text: "Go to GOV.UK One Login", href:"create-or-sign-in" }) }}
Benefits of GOV.UK One Login

A GOV.UK One Login allows you to access many government services using the same email address and password. It also makes your Companies House account more secure.

If you use the same email address for your GOV.UK One Login as your existing Companies House account, we'll save any information stored, such as:

  • your filing history
  • companies you follow

Other ways to sign in

For now, if you still access your Companies House account without signing in to GOV.UK One Login, you can sign in with existing Companies House account details instead.

In the future, you will need to create a GOV.UK One Login to use all Companies House online services.
